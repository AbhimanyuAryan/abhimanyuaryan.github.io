Spoke at **Entrepreneur Summit Hyderabad 2019** on the challenges of taking **Virtual Reality** to the masses.

The talk explored why large-scale VR adoption is hard today, and why **Augmented Reality** plus **AI-enabled** use cases are often more customer-friendly and easier to scale for everyday users, such as **AR Google Maps** style experiences.
