Gave a talk at the **Voxel51 AI, Machine Learning and Computer Vision Meetup** on how knowledge graphs can reduce hallucinations in ChatGPT and similar AI systems.

The talk demonstrated using **LangChain** and **Neo4j** to ground LLM outputs in structured knowledge, showing how graph-based retrieval produces more accurate and contextually grounded responses compared to vanilla RAG approaches.

Alongside great talks from Harpreet Sahota (Data-Centric AI Competition on Hugging Face) and Tim Spann (Sensor Data in RAG with Milvus).
